Colour
Dogs are in different colours. Generally, they are brown, black and red. Some are white, others are spotted. So they widely differ in colour.
Size
They are different in size also. Some dogs are very short. Some are lean and thin. Some are very strong. Generally, a dog is two feet in height. English dogs are bulky and strong.
Food
Dogs eat up everything except fruits and vegetables. They are very fond of eating meat and bread. They can also drink tea and milk. Sometimes they kill small birds like parrots and pigeon for food. They can also eat rats and cats.
Habits
A dog is a very faithful animal. It recognizes its masters. Seeing him it began to wag its tail. It becomes alert even at the slightest noise. Seeing a stranger, it starts barking. It is very sincere and honest.
